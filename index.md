Hi Everyone!
My name is Iqra, and I am an engineering student at UET Lahore. My studies mostly focus on how software and hardware work together. I spend a lot of my time working on subjects like Machine Learning, Data Science, and Digital Logic Design. For me, engineering is not just about reading books it is about building things and solving real problems using code and logic.

**My Interests**

Outside of my lab work, I am really interested in exploring the world of Artificial Intelligence and Data Visualization. I enjoy taking messy data and turning it into clear, visual stories that anyone can understand. I also like spending time on hardware-level logic and seeing how small circuit designs can power big ideas. Exploring new tools on GitHub and keeping my technical blog updated are some of the ways I stay curious and keep growing as a student.

**Purpose behind my blog**

I started this blog to keep a record of everything I am learning. Here, I share my lab reports, technical projects, and the challenges I face along the way. Technology changes very fast, so I use this space to document my progress and stay updated.
