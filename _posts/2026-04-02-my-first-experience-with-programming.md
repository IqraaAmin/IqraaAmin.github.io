## Learning Basic Programming Concepts

In Programming Fundamentals (PF), I learned basic concepts of programming such as variables, data types, and simple conditions. These concepts helped me understand how a computer follows instructions step by step. It was the beginning of my journey into the world of coding.

## Writing My First Program in PF Lab

During the PF lab, I wrote and executed my first simple program. Seeing the program run successfully gave me confidence and motivation to learn more. It showed how theory can be applied practically.

## Debugging and Fixing Errors

While writing programs, I faced errors and mistakes in my code. Which sometimes feel difficult. Learning how to identify and fix these errors helped me understand logic better.
