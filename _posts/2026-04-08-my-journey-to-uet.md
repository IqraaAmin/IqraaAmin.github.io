# My Journey to UET Faisalabad

![Uet FSD Campus](uet pic.jfif)

My name is Iqra, and I am from Faisalabad, Pakistan. After completing my ICS, I reached one of the most important turning points of my life—choosing a career and a university that would shape my future. Since childhood, I had a strong interest in technology, computers, and innovation. I was always curious about how computers work and how technology is transforming the world. Because of this passion, I decided to pursue Computer Engineering as my career.

After completing my intermediate studies, I started exploring different universities and degree programs. I wanted to choose a field that matched my interests and a university that would provide quality education and opportunities for growth. During this period, I appeared in two entry tests: one for an Agricultural University and the other for UET Faisalabad. Although both options offered promising futures, I felt that engineering was the right field for me. After researching different universities and considering my goals, I found UET Faisalabad to be the best choice for pursuing Computer Engineering. Its academic reputation, engineering programs, and learning environment motivated me to make it my first preference.

## Preparing for the Entry Tests

Preparing for the entry tests was one of the most challenging phases of my academic journey. The tests required a strong understanding of Mathematics, Physics, and English. Since the competition was tough, I knew that I had to work hard to achieve my goal.

I followed a strict study schedule and dedicated several hours every day to preparation. I revised important concepts, solved practice questions, and worked on past papers to improve my understanding and speed. There were days when I felt exhausted because balancing preparation with other responsibilities was not easy. Sometimes difficult questions made me doubt myself, but I remained determined and continued working hard.

My family played a very important role during this time. They constantly encouraged me and reminded me to stay focused on my goals. Their support gave me confidence and helped me overcome stress and anxiety. Whenever I felt discouraged, I remembered my dream of becoming a computer engineer and studying at UET Faisalabad. This motivation kept me moving forward despite all challenges.

As the examination dates approached, my nervousness increased. I wanted to perform well in both tests and secure admission to a reputable university. On the day of the exams, I entered the examination hall with confidence and determination. I tried my best to remain calm and focused while attempting every question carefully.

## The Day the Results Were Announced

After completing the entry tests, the waiting period began. It felt like one of the longest periods of my life because I was constantly thinking about the results. Every day, I wondered whether my hard work would be rewarded and whether I would be able to achieve my dream.

Finally, the results were announced. I was extremely nervous and excited at the same time. My heart was beating fast as I searched for my name on the merit list. I checked the result several times because I could hardly believe what I was seeing. When I finally found my name among the selected candidates for UET Faisalabad, I felt an overwhelming sense of happiness and relief.

It was one of the proudest moments of my life. All the hard work, dedication, and sacrifices I had made during my preparation had finally paid off. I immediately shared the good news with my family, and their joy made the moment even more special. Seeing the pride and happiness on their faces was a feeling I will never forget.

Although I had appeared in both entry tests and had more than one option available, I carefully considered my interests and future ambitions. My passion had always been related to technology and engineering, so choosing UET Faisalabad felt like the right decision. I was confident that it would provide me with the knowledge and opportunities needed to achieve my goals.

## Starting a New Chapter

Getting admission to UET Faisalabad marked the beginning of a completely new chapter in my life. I was excited to start my university journey, meet new people, and experience a different learning environment. At the same time, I felt a little nervous because university life is very different from school and college life.

I had heard that engineering is a challenging field that requires dedication, hard work, and problem-solving skills. However, instead of feeling afraid, I saw these challenges as opportunities to grow and improve myself. I was determined to work hard, develop my technical skills, and make the most of every opportunity available to me.

University education is not only about earning a degree; it is also about personal growth, learning new skills, and preparing for a professional career. Therefore, I entered UET Faisalabad with a positive mindset and a strong commitment to achieving success. I wanted to gain knowledge, build confidence, and become a skilled computer engineer who could contribute to society through technology and innovation.

Looking back, the journey from completing ICS to securing admission to UET Faisalabad taught me many valuable lessons. It taught me the importance of perseverance, patience, self-confidence, and hard work. It also showed me the value of family support and determination in achieving one's goals.

Today, I am proud of my decision and grateful for the opportunities that have come my way. My admission to UET Faisalabad was not just an academic achievement; it was the realization of a dream that I had worked hard to achieve. As I continue my studies in Computer Engineering, I remain committed to learning, improving, and striving for excellence. I believe that with dedication, determination, and continuous effort, I can achieve my goals and build a successful future.
